Welcome to IIC

KEC IIC Activity Management is an online platform for managing various activities and events organized by the KEC IIC. Our goal is to provide a seamless experience to the students, staff, and faculty members of KEC.

Our platform enables the organizers to create and manage different activities and events, register participants, and track their progress. The participants can also view the details of the events and interact with other participants.
